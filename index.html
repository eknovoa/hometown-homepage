Top 3 activities to do in Houston

Attend a baseball game.

A Houston Astro's baseball game is a great way to spend an afternoon or evening.

Go to the Houston Rodeo.

A trip to Texas is not complete without the rodeo. You can watch rodeo events and musical performances, and eat some incredible food.

Visit the Johnson Space Center.

A must visit for fans of space exploration.
